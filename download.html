Installing from browser

Since the app is being installed from your browser and not the Play Store, Google Play Protect may prompt you to scan the APK. Simply tap “More details” then choose “Install anyway” to continue.

Squarre is currently only available as a mobile app. However, we've built it with multiplatform support so you can access the web version on iOS or desktop.
